# Feature Suggestions for Elder Care Management System

Based on industry best practices and common needs in elder care facilities, here are feature ideas organized by priority and category.

##  High Priority Features

### 1. **Incident Reports / Incidentes**
- **What**: Track falls, injuries, accidents, behavioral incidents
- **Why**: Critical for legal compliance and resident safety
- **Features**:
  - Date/time, location, description
  - Staff witness reports
  - Photos/documentation
  - Follow-up actions taken
  - Family notification tracking
  - Export for insurance/legal

### 2. **Daily Care Notes / Notas Diarias**
- **What**: Daily activity logs for each resident
- **Why**: Track daily care, meals, activities, mood
- **Features**:
  - Meal intake (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  - Bathing/hygiene notes
  - Sleep patterns
  - Mood/behavior observations
  - Activities participated in
  - General notes section

### 3. **Family Portal / Portal Familiar**
- **What**: Secure access for family members to view resident info
- **Why**: Improves communication and transparency
- **Features**:
  - View medications, appointments, vital signs
  - Receive notifications
  - Message staff
  - View photos/updates
  - Read-only access with permissions

### 4. **Notifications & Alerts / Notificaciones**
- **What**: Push notifications and email alerts
- **Why**: Critical for medication reminders and urgent alerts
- **Features**:
  - Medication due reminders
  - Appointment reminders
  - Abnormal vital signs alerts
  - Missed medication alerts
  - Low medication stock alerts
  - Email/SMS notifications

### 5. **Reports & Analytics / Reportes**
- **What**: Generate comprehensive reports
- **Why**: Required for compliance and decision-making
- **Features**:
  - Medication adherence reports
  - Vital signs trends
  - Incident reports
  - Billing statements
  - Monthly care summaries
  - Export to PDF/Excel

##  UI/UX Improvements

- **Better Mobile Experience**: Larger touch targets, swipe gestures
- **Accessibility**: Screen reader support, high contrast mode
- **Multi-language**: Already have Spanish/English, could add more
- **Customizable Themes**: Let facilities brand the app
- **Tutorial/Onboarding**: Help new users learn the system

##  Notes

- Consider HIPAA compliance for all features
- Ensure all features work offline or have graceful degradation
- Test on tablets (common in care facilities)
- Keep bilingual support for all new features
- Consider printing needs (many facilities still use paper)
