Comprehensive Features

Everything you need to manage elderly care facilities efficiently and professionally

Dashboard

Real-time overview of all critical information including medications, appointments, and vital signs. Get instant insights into resident health status.

Residents Management

Complete resident profiles with personal information, medical history, emergency contacts, and photo management. Track all residents in one place.

Documents

Upload and store resident documents (scanner PDFs or photos). Download documents anytime for insurance, audits, or medical visits.

Training (Practice Mode)

Train caregivers and admins inside the app using demo data hidden from normal views. Generate Practice Reports (PDF) using all training residents with a date range.

Medications Tracking

Schedule and track medication administration with automated alerts. Monitor adherence and generate medication reports for healthcare providers.

Appointments

Manage medical appointments, therapy sessions, and family visits. Set reminders and track appointment history for each resident.

Vital Signs

Track blood pressure, glucose levels, and weight with interactive charts. Includes color-coded health indicators and trends over time.

Calendar

Unified calendar view of all appointments, medication schedules, and important events. Export to Google Calendar for easy access.

Billing & Financial

Comprehensive billing system with invoice generation, payment tracking, and financial reporting. Manage accounts receivable efficiently.

Incidents Management

Document and track incidents with detailed reporting. Generate incident reports and maintain compliance records for regulatory requirements.

Daily Care Notes

Record daily care activities including meals, mobility, pain levels, sleep quality, and mood. Comprehensive documentation for continuity of care.

Notifications

Automated alerts for medications, appointments, and critical events. Supports email notifications and optional SMS via carrier gateways.

Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports on medications, appointments, vital signs, and financial data. Export to PDF for record keeping.

Staff Management

Manage staff accounts, roles, and permissions. Track staff assignments and maintain secure access controls.

History & Journal

View activity history by resident, date range, and staff. Export or print reports for audits and compliance.

Time Clock (PIN)

Clock in, start/end breaks, and clock out using a staff PIN. Supports fast login workflows for caregivers.

Archived Residents

Archive inactive residents safely (soft delete) and restore them later. Protects historical records and avoids accidental data loss.

Reports Export (PDF/CSV)

Generate printable reports and export data to PDF or CSV. Email reports when needed for physicians, families, or internal records.

Regulations

Quick access to facility regulations and reference materials to support compliance and staff training.

Settings

Configure facility preferences, roles, and system options. Designed for administrators and managers.

Financial Management

Admin tools for billing workflows, receipts, and reconciliation. Track balances, payments, and generate summaries.
