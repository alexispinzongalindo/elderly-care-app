# Care Notes - Recommended Information

## Current Fields (Already Implemented ✅)

1. **Date & Shift** - When care was provided
2. **Meals** - Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
3. **Personal Care** - Bathing, Hygiene
4. **Sleep** - Hours slept, Quality
5. **Mood & Behavior** - Mood selection, Behavior notes
6. **Activities** - Activities participated in
7. **General Notes** - Any other observations

## Recommended Additional Fields (Missing ⚠️)

### Critical for Elder Care:

1. **Toileting/Incontinence** 
   - Frequency, type, any issues
   - Essential for tracking health and hygiene

2. **Mobility/Ambulation**
   - How resident moved (walked, wheelchair, bedbound)
   - Distance or duration
   - Any assistance needed

3. **Pain Assessment**
   - Pain level (0-10 scale or mild/moderate/severe)
   - Location of pain
   - Pain management given

4. **Fluid Intake**
   - Total fluid consumed
   - Type of fluids
   - Important for hydration tracking

5. **Appetite Rating**
   - Poor, Fair, Good, Excellent
   - Helps track eating patterns

6. **Skin Condition**
   - Any redness, sores, wounds
   - Wound care provided
   - Pressure point assessment

7. **Social Interaction**
   - Visits received
   - Phone calls
   - Social activities

8. **Fall Risk Assessment**
   - Any falls or near-falls
   - Safety measures taken

9. **Time Field**
   - Specific time (in addition to date)
   - Helps track when care was provided

10. **Cognitive Status**
    - Alertness level
    - Orientation (time, place, person)
    - Memory observations
