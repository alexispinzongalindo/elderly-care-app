Use Staff PIN (Clock In & Login) or Admin username/password.

Choose a resident to unlock resident-specific pages. On mobile, you may be prompted once per session to confirm.
3) Record Care
Use Medications, Appointments, Care Notes, Incidents, and Vital Signs to document daily care.
4) Vital Signs
Record BP, glucose, temperature, heart rate, weight, plus respiratory rate, SpO‚ÇÇ, oxygen details, and pain score.
